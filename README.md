# IndexTranche.lu

**[indextranche.lu](https://www.indextranche.lu)** is a free, non-commercial reference tool for Luxembourg's wage index (*échelle mobile des salaires*) — a mechanism that automatically adjusts all salaries, wages and pensions by 2.5% whenever inflation crosses a fixed threshold.

## Mission

The index affects every worker and employer in Luxembourg, yet official data is scattered across STATEC publications and hard to interpret at a glance. IndexTranche.lu brings it together in one place:

- **Live tracking** — current application rate, next trigger threshold, and forecast date for the next tranche
- **Interactive chart** — full historical evolution of the index since 1988
- **Salary calculator** — estimate the impact of an upcoming tranche on any gross salary
- **Complete history** — every tranche since 1988 with dates and rates, sourced from STATEC
- **In-depth blog** — long-form articles on the history (1921–today), the 2022–2023 inflation crisis, the ongoing political controversies, and the index's future

All data comes directly from official STATEC publications. The site is purely informational — no financial advice, no ads, no tracking.
